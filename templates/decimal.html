1\ Be sure to copy this decimal form of your private keys to the clipboard and also on a seperate file (perferably offline). It is imperative that you keep this in a safe and secure place. Without these private keys you will not be able to access your bitcoins in the future. This site does not store any private keys, public keys, nor public addresses.

2\ Private keys can be represented in multiple forms. The decimal format can be transformed into a hexadecimal format, BIP38 format, or even a WIF format. The WIF format is the suggested way to keep your private keys as most wallet softwares accept this format when importing your UTXOs.
